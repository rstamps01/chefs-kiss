# Crunchtime Research Findings

**URL:** https://www.crunchtime.com/
**Company:** Crunchtime Information Systems, Inc.
**Market Position:** Enterprise-level restaurant operations management for multi-unit restaurants

## Key Features

### 1. Inventory Management
- Automated sales forecasting and purchasing
- Faster inventory counting
- Recipe management
- Reconciliation capabilities
- Food cost variance tracking (7% reduction claimed)

### 2. Labor & Scheduling
- Employee scheduling app
- Labor cost management (2% savings claimed)
- Labor law compliance
- Real-time labor tracking
- Predictive scheduling

### 3. Operations Execution
- Temperature monitoring
- Food prep labeling
- Kitchen display system
- Guest management
- Task management and checklists
- 50% less employee turnover claimed

### 4. Learning & Development
- Restaurant training courses
- Employee onboarding

## Key Differentiators

## Gaps Identified
- No explicit mention of weather impact analysis

- No mention of external data integration (weather, events, foot traffic)
